Easy Interview - Voice Input

Look for the voice input button in the bottom right corner of the ChatGPT page.

Supported Languages:

  • Ukrainian
  • Russian
  • English (US)

How to Use:

  1. Select your preferred language from the dropdown
  2. Click the microphone button or press Ctrl + X to start recording
  3. Speak your message
  4. Click the button again or press Ctrl + X to stop recording
  5. Click the send button to submit your message

Note: The text will automatically clear after sending your message.
